The Best Thing About Our Flying Ambulance Is Where It Lands

When serious injuries happen, time matters. And expertise is invaluable. So our flying ambulance, ThedaStar, brings patients to our Level II Trauma Center in Neenah, a facility known for handling tough situations. Our Trauma Center treats more patients with severe injuries than most Level I and Level II Trauma Centers nationwide. With better results. Our mortality rate is lower, and only 0.4% of all patients require a transfer to another healthcare system. ThedaCare’s Trauma Center provides 24-hour coverage by board-certified emergency medical physicians and specialists. When precious lives hang in the balance, ThedaCare rises to the challenge.

Collaboration Brings Peace of Mind

Our approach to cancer care includes lots of time at home, because that’s where you feel most comfortable. Technology and expertise play important roles. But so do family, friends and familiar surroundings. So our treatment centers are close to where you live. And even if your cancer is rare and difficult to treat, our membership in the Mayo Clinic Care Network means ThedaCare specialists can consult with Mayo Clinic on your care, with the goal of keeping you close to home and the people you love. As the first health care system in Wisconsin to be selected for the Mayo Clinic Care Network, we have access to online medical resources that provide the latest Mayo-vetted knowledge in disease management, clinical care guidelines and treatment recommendations. And the network works. Since 2013, more than 200 eConsults have been submitted for complex cases. 86% were treated locally.

A Team Approach

Our cancer therapy is designed to provide understanding and support to help you and your caregivers face the challenges of fighting cancer. Throughout your treatment journey, we’re all on the same team. Electronic medical records means we share common information. And our doctors share thoughts, observations and opinions with one another. So everyone- surgeons, genetic counselors, oncologists, behavioral therapists, nutritionists, and your family doctor- is on the same team and on the same page, to ensure that you get the best treatment plan possible. At ThedaCare, you’ll always have an informed choice in your care plan. This focus on teamwork is important to us, because we know it works.

The new ThedaCare regional cancer center is set to open in Appleton in 2016, and this collaborative approach is at the core of its design. By including all areas of expertise at the facility- oncology, behavioral health, nutrition, therapy and more-we’re designing a cancer center for the future, for you.
